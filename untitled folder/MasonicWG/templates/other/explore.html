How We Defend Freedom

FRDM is dedicated to protecting constitutional rights through a multi-faceted approach. Our work is centered on three core pillars: legal defense, education, and legislative advocacy.

Through our Legal Defense Fund, we support landmark constitutional cases in courts across the country, fighting to protect the rights of all Americans. Our Educational Programs empower citizens, including the next generation, with a deep understanding of their rights and the importance of civic engagement. Finally, our Legislative Advocacy team works tirelessly at the federal and state levels to monitor legislation and champion laws that safeguard our freedoms.

Members are the backbone of our efforts and gain access to exclusive benefits, including our monthly publications, special discounts, and invitations to community events. By joining FRDM, you directly support these vital programs.
