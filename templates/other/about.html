Our Mission and History

Our mission is to protect and defend the constitutional rights of all Americans through advocacy, education, and an unwavering commitment to freedom. We are founded on the core values of constitutional protection, community unity, and civic awareness.

Established in 1971 by a group of concerned citizens, FRDM has grown from a passionate grassroots movement into America's leading voice for constitutional rights. Over the decades, we have established a robust legal defense fund to support landmark cases and adapted to the digital age to provide educational resources to millions. Today, with over 5 million members nationwide, we continue to fight for the principles of liberty and justice for all.
