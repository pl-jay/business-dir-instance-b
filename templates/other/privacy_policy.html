Our Commitment to Your Privacy

This is a simplified overview of our privacy policy. For full details, please contact our privacy officer.

What Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us when you become a member, make a donation, or contact us. This includes your name, address, email, and payment details. When you visit our website, we also automatically collect technical information, such as your IP address and browsing activity, to help us improve our services.

How We Use and Share Your Information

Your information is used to manage your membership, process payments, send you communications and publications, and provide you with member benefits. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with trusted service providers who help us operate (like payment processors and mail houses) or when legally required to do so. These partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Your Rights and Data Security

We implement strong security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your data from unauthorized access. You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal information. You can also opt-out of marketing communications at any time through your member portal or by using the "unsubscribe" link in our emails.
