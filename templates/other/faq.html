Common Questions

How do I become a FRDM member?

Joining is easy! You can sign up on our membership page, where you'll find several options, including annual, multi-year, and life memberships. You can also call our membership services at (800) 672-3888 to join over the phone.

What are the main benefits of membership?

As a member, you'll receive a subscription to one of our monthly magazines, access to exclusive discounts on training and gear, invitations to member-only events, and the knowledge that you are supporting our critical legal and educational programs.

How can I update my account information?

The best way to update your address or contact information is by logging into your online member portal. You can also contact our member services team by emailing membership@frdm.org or calling (800) 672-3888 during business hours.
